Our Food

Our coffee is a unique 3 bean blend that our founders spent a great deal of time and effort to find, resulting in the perfect combination for our customer's palate, and the easy-drinking, flavourful taste that we now have.

Although we serve exceptionally good coffee, and quite a lot of it, we are best known for our lush and inviting food.

Our food is fresh and prepared in our own kitchens every morning. At the end of the day, if any of these items still remain in the fridges, we offer them to those in need and start all over again the following morning.

For items that we cannot make ourselves, we pride ourselves in offering a wide assortment of snacks and treats for every customer's indulgence.

With that being said, we are always looking for lovely new suppliers of artisanal, homemade goods. We like to think of ourselves as a bit of an 'urban farm-store', so please ccontact us if you have any great new products or ideas.
